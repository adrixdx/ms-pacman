    Description of the game

    You perform Mr. Pacman wife, Ms. Pacman, and you have to eat all the pellets over the laberinth fleeing the ghosts, level by level, in this addictive game. But Ms. Pacman can eat the Power Pellets located all over the laberinth to fight the ghosts, earning for a few seconds the capacity of eat her enemies and return them to the central zone of the laberinth. Try to earn the maximum score until the ghosts catch you.

    In this version you have new modes that you can activate, like the "God Mode" that makes un invincible, the "Collisions Mode" to see the collisions of the objects in the game, or even the "Direction Mode" to see the positions where the gh9osts gonna go to catch Ms. Pacman. (For more information, read the README.txt added in the download)
